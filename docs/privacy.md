# Privacy

Everything Audio Looper processes audio locally in your browser.

## What Is Collected

Nothing is collected by this app in v1.

## Audio Recordings

Recordings are decoded, analyzed, sliced, and played on your device. They are not uploaded to a server by the application.

If you choose the Librosa WASM analyzer, the browser may download Pyodide/librosa runtime files from public package/CDN hosts. Your recorded audio is still processed inside the browser worker.

## Local Storage

Saved kits and preferences are stored in your browser using IndexedDB and localStorage. You can clear them using your browser site-data controls.

## Analytics

No analytics are included in v1.
